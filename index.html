Biography

I am a final-year undergraduate student in the IEEE Honor Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, majoring in Computer Science. I am fortunately advised by Professor Weinan Zhang in Apex Lab. I currently intern at Harvard's Kempner Institute, where I work under the guidance of Professor Yilun Du. Previously, I have gained valuable experience at Shanghai AI Lab and ByteDance Seed. Currently, my research interest includes reinforcement learning, robotics and sim-to-real. I am also open to explore other research topics. Feel free to reach out via email.
